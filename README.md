Archive of Extinct Constellations
An observatory built for stars that no longer exist.
This place no longer records the movement of planets.
The telescopes have rusted.
The maps have faded.
The names of entire constellations have been forgotten.
Yet somehow, the light remains.
This repository exists for the stories that refused to disappear.
For worlds that never truly ended.
For memories that continue travelling long after their stars have died.
 
⸻
 
Observatory Log
The sea keeps many things.
Broken promises.
Unfinished conversations.
Entire worlds swallowed beneath its surface.
I suppose I am not very different.
I collect fragments.
Pieces of stories.
Characters who lingered longer than they should have.
Moments that hurt just enough to become beautiful.
If this observatory serves any purpose at all,
perhaps it is simply to remember.
 
⸻
 
“Some things are lost forever.

Others merely drift beyond the horizon.”
